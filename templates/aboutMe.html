My Live...

Hi, I’m William, a systems engineer with a great passion for technology, software development, and cybersecurity. Throughout my journey, I’ve worked with technologies like Java (Spring Boot), .NET, Python, and Docker, building everything from REST APIs to complete microservices, always focused on quality, scalability, and best practices.

I see myself as a committed, proactive person with a strong desire to keep learning and growing, both professionally and personally. I enjoy working in teams, sharing knowledge, and contributing to a collaborative environment where ideas can flow and projects can evolve solidly.

I currently live in Germany, where in addition to continuing my specialization in software engineering. I’m learning German to better integrate and expand my opportunities. Beyond code. I deeply value honesty, responsibility, and family balance — pillars that drive me every day.

I’m also open to collaborating as a freelancer, offering my knowledge and experience to those who need to develop a digital product, optimize processes, or create tailored solutions. If you have a project in mind or are looking for someone to help bring your ideas to the next level, feel free to reach out. I’d love to join you on that journey!
